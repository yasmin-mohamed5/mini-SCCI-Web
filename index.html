SCCI – Student Community for Creativity & Innovation

is a university community that brings together students from different faculties and helps them develop their skills in technology, media, business, and entrepreneurship. Through workshops, training programs, and real projects, SCCI provides a practical learning environment where students can gain hands-on experience and build a strong foundation before entering the job market.
